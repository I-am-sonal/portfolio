ORRA Jewellery is a chain of jewellery stores in India which also sells jewellery online.[1][2][3][4][5][6][7] It has 34 stores in 25 cities and employs over 450 people all over India. It is headed by Shri Ram College and S.P. Jain Institute of Managment alumni Vijay Jain.[8] ORRA has been awarded Best Platinum jeweller of the year in 2013 and has won India’s Most Trusted Diamond Jewellery Brand for three years consecutively.[9] In 2016 it was picked up by Google for a case study
